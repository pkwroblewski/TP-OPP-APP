# Luxembourg GAAP Profit & Loss Account (Compte de Profits et Pertes) - Line Item Reference

## Format Options
Luxembourg allows vertical or horizontal formats. Most common is vertical format by nature of expense.

---

## VERTICAL FORMAT (BY NATURE)

### 1. Net Turnover (Montant net du chiffre d'affaires)
- French: "Montant net du chiffre d'affaires"
- Revenue from ordinary activities
- Net of VAT, returns, discounts

### 2. Variation in Stocks (Variation des stocks)
- French: "Variation des stocks de produits finis et en cours"
- Increase/decrease in finished goods and WIP

### 3. Work Performed and Capitalized (Production immobilisée)
- French: "Travaux effectués par l'entreprise pour elle-même et portés à l'actif"

### 4. Other Operating Income (Autres produits d'exploitation)
- French: "Autres produits d'exploitation"
- **May include IC service fees** if not in turnover
- **May include IC management fees**
- Check notes for breakdown

### 5. Raw Materials and Consumables (Matières premières et consommables)
- French: "Matières premières et consommables et autres charges externes"
- a) Raw materials and consumables
- b) Other external charges

### 6. Staff Costs (Frais de personnel)
- French: "Frais de personnel"
- a) Wages and salaries - "Salaires et traitements"
- b) Social security costs - "Charges sociales"
   - (i) Pensions - "dont sur les pensions"

### 7. Value Adjustments (Corrections de valeur)
- French: "Corrections de valeur"
- a) On formation expenses and tangible/intangible fixed assets
- b) On current assets

### 8. Other Operating Charges (Autres charges d'exploitation)
- French: "Autres charges d'exploitation"
- May include IC service fees paid

### 9. Income from Participating Interests (Produits provenant de participations)
- French: "Produits provenant de participations"
- **May include IC dividends** - check notes
- a) From affiliated undertakings - "provenant d'entreprises liées"

### 10. Income from Other Investments and Loans (Produits provenant d'autres placements)
- French: "Produits provenant d'autres placements et créances de l'actif immobilisé"
- **KEY IC ITEM**: Often IC interest income
- a) **From affiliated undertakings** - "provenant d'entreprises liées"
- **THIS IS THE PRIMARY LOCATION FOR IC INTEREST INCOME**

### 11. Other Interest Receivable (Autres intérêts et produits assimilés)
- French: "Autres intérêts et autres produits assimilés"
- **May include IC interest** - check notes
- a) **From affiliated undertakings** - "provenant d'entreprises liées"

### 12. Share in Profit/Loss of Undertakings Accounted for Under Equity Method
- French: "Quote-part de résultat provenant des entreprises mises en équivalence"

### 13. Value Adjustments on Financial Assets (Corrections de valeur sur immobilisations financières)
- French: "Corrections de valeur sur immobilisations financières et valeurs mobilières"

### 14. Interest Payable (Intérêts et charges assimilées)
- French: "Intérêts et charges assimilées"
- **KEY IC ITEM**: Often IC interest expense
- a) **Concerning affiliated undertakings** - "concernant des entreprises liées"
- **THIS IS THE PRIMARY LOCATION FOR IC INTEREST EXPENSE**

### 15. Tax on Profit or Loss (Impôts sur le résultat)
- French: "Impôts sur le résultat de l'exercice"

### 16. Profit or Loss After Taxation (Résultat après impôts)
- French: "Résultat après impôts de l'exercice"

### 17. Other Taxes Not Shown Under Items Above
- French: "Autres impôts ne figurant pas sous les postes ci-dessus"

### 18. Profit or Loss for the Financial Year (Résultat de l'exercice)
- French: "Résultat de l'exercice"

## KEY INTER-COMPANY REVENUE/EXPENSE ITEMS

### IC Revenue Items:

#### 1. IC Service/Management Fee Income
**P&L Location:**
- Item 4: "Autres produits d'exploitation" (Other Operating Income)

**Typical Note Disclosure:**
"Other operating income includes management fees charged to affiliated undertakings of EUR X."

**What to Extract:**
- Amount charged to group companies
- Description of services (management, administrative, technical)
- Source: "P&L Item 4, Note X, page Y"

#### 2. IC Interest Income
**Primary P&L Location:**
- Item 10: "Produits provenant d'autres placements et créances de l'actif immobilisé"
  - Sub-item 10a: "provenant d'entreprises liées" (from affiliated undertakings)

**Alternative P&L Location:**
- Item 11: "Autres intérêts et autres produits assimilés"
  - Sub-item 11a: "provenant d'entreprises liées"

**Typical Note Disclosure:**
"Income from other investments comprises interest on loans to affiliated undertakings of EUR X."

**What to Extract:**
- Interest amount from group companies
- Related loan principal (cross-reference balance sheet)
- Calculate implied interest rate if possible
- Source: "P&L Item 10a, Note X, page Y"

#### 3. IC Dividend Income
**P&L Location:**
- Item 9: "Produits provenant de participations"
  - Sub-item 9a: "provenant d'entreprises liées"

**What to Extract:**
- Dividend amount from subsidiaries/group companies
- Source: "P&L Item 9a, Note X, page Y"

### IC Expense Items:

#### 1. IC Service/Management Fees Paid
**P&L Location:**
- Item 5b: "Autres charges externes" (Other external charges)
- OR Item 8: "Autres charges d'exploitation" (Other operating charges)

**Typical Note Disclosure:**
"Other external charges include EUR X for management fees paid to affiliated undertakings."

**What to Extract:**
- Amount paid to group companies
- Description of services received
- Source: "P&L Item 5b or 8, Note X, page Y"

#### 2. IC Interest Expense
**P&L Location:**
- Item 14: "Intérêts et charges assimilées" (Interest payable)
  - Sub-item 14a: "concernant des entreprises liées" (concerning affiliated undertakings)

**Typical Note Disclosure:**
"Interest payable includes EUR X concerning affiliated undertakings."

**What to Extract:**
- Interest amount paid to group companies
- Related loan principal (cross-reference balance sheet)
- Calculate implied interest rate if possible
- Source: "P&L Item 14a, Note X, page Y"

## NOTES DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS

### Mandatory Note Disclosures (Per Luxembourg GAAP):
Luxembourg Company Law requires separate disclosure in notes of:
- Income from affiliated undertakings (interest, dividends, fees)
- Expenses concerning affiliated undertakings (interest, fees)
- Breakdown of "Other operating income" if material
- Breakdown of "Other operating charges" if material

### Typical Note Structure:

**Note X - Other Operating Income:**
```
Other operating income comprises:
- Management fees charged to ABC Sister Co     EUR 50,000
- Administrative services to XYZ Subsidiary    EUR 25,000
- Rental income from group companies           EUR 15,000
Total                                          EUR 90,000
```

**Note X - Income from Other Investments:**
```
Income from other investments and loans comprises interest on:
- Loan to ABC Sister Co (5-year term, 3.5%)   EUR 67,800
```

**Note X - Interest Payable:**
```
Interest payable includes:
- Interest on loan from Parent Co              EUR 40,000
```

## EXTRACTION METHODOLOGY

### Step 1: Identify IC Items in P&L

```
Scan P&L for these specific items:

☑ Item 4 - Check if broken down in notes → IC services revenue
☑ Item 9a - "provenant d'entreprises liées" → IC dividends
☑ Item 10a - "provenant d'entreprises liées" → IC interest income
☑ Item 11a - "provenant d'entreprises liées" → IC interest income (additional)
☑ Item 14a - "concernant des entreprises liées" → IC interest expense
```

### Step 2: Read Referenced Notes

```
For each identified item, find the corresponding note:

Example:
P&L Item 10: EUR 100,000 (Note 8)
→ Read Note 8
→ Extract: "Interest on loans to affiliated undertakings: EUR 67,800"
→ Do NOT extract the total EUR 100,000
→ Extract ONLY the EUR 67,800 with proper source
```

### Step 3: Cross-Reference with Balance Sheet

```
Validation check:
If P&L shows IC interest income of EUR 67,800:
→ Balance sheet should show IC loan provided
→ Calculate: 67,800 / Loan Balance = Implied Rate
→ If no IC loan found → ERROR in extraction
→ If IC loan exists but no interest → FLAG as zero-spread opportunity
```

### Step 4: Document Sources

```
Every extracted amount must include:
{
  "amount": 67800,
  "source": "P&L Item 10a - Income from other investments, Note 8, page 12",
  "description": "Interest income on IC loan to ABC Sister Co",
  "cross_reference": "Balance sheet IC loan: EUR 2,000,000 (Note 6, page 8)",
  "implied_rate": "3.39%",
  "verified": true
}
```

## COMMON EXTRACTION PITFALLS

### ❌ ERROR 1: Extracting Total Instead of IC Portion
**Problem:**
```
P&L Item 10: Produits provenant d'autres placements    EUR 100,000
Note 8: Of which interest from affiliated undertakings EUR 67,800
```
**Wrong:** Extract EUR 100,000
**Correct:** Extract EUR 67,800 with source "P&L Item 10, Note 8"

### ❌ ERROR 2: Mixing Operating and Financial Items
**Problem:** Treating management fees as interest or vice versa
**Solution:**
- Management/service fees → Item 4 (Operating income)
- Interest income → Items 10 or 11 (Financial income)
- Interest expense → Item 14 (Financial charges)

### ❌ ERROR 3: Ignoring Note References
**Problem:** Only reading P&L line items, missing IC breakdowns
**Solution:** ALWAYS check notes for items 4, 9, 10, 11, 14

### ❌ ERROR 4: Assuming Non-Existent IC Items
**Problem:** AI hallucinates IC transactions not in the accounts
**Example:** "Service fees EUR 91.3M" when no such amount exists
**Solution:** Extract ONLY amounts explicitly stated as IC in P&L or notes

### ❌ ERROR 5: No Cross-Validation
**Problem:** Extracting IC interest without checking for corresponding loan
**Solution:** Always validate:
- IC interest income → Should have IC loan provided on balance sheet
- IC interest expense → Should have IC loan received on balance sheet

## ZERO-SPREAD DETECTION LOGIC

### Critical Validation:
```
IF (Balance Sheet shows IC loan provided > EUR 0)
  AND (P&L Items 10a + 11a = EUR 0 OR not disclosed)
THEN
  FLAG: "ZERO-SPREAD IC FINANCING OPPORTUNITY"
  PRIORITY: HIGH
  
IF (Balance Sheet shows IC loan received > EUR 0)
  AND (P&L Item 14a = EUR 0 OR not disclosed)
THEN
  FLAG: "ZERO-SPREAD IC FINANCING (BORROWER SIDE)"
  PRIORITY: MEDIUM (less common TP issue)
```

### Example:

## BEST PRACTICES SUMMARY

1. **Always read sub-items**: Item 10 vs Item 10a makes all the difference
2. **Always read notes**: Most IC detail is in notes, not on P&L face
3. **Cross-validate**: IC income/expense should match balance sheet positions
4. **Calculate implied rates**: Interest / Loan Balance = Reality check
5. **Source everything**: Every number needs "Item X, Note Y, page Z"
6. **Flag anomalies**: Zero-spread, extreme rates, vague descriptions
7. **Don't hallucinate**: If it's not explicitly stated as IC, don't extract it
